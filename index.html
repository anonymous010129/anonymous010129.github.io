ConRet Web Demo

Retrieval-augmented audio-conditioned generation for both video-to-audio and text-to-audio. This site packages the shared peer-review subset currently included in the repository.

Shared review subset. 5 dataset-check examples, 10 V2A samples, and 5 T2A samples are available for direct listening on the web.

Overview

ConRet is a retrieval-augmented audio-conditioned generator on both modalities, and the first audio-conditioned model on the text-to-audio side. Earlier AC-V2A systems train with a slice of the ground-truth audio itself as the reference, which creates a train-test gap. ConRet replaces self-reference with retrieval-sampled references and adds a multi-way contrastive forward so the model must produce distinguishable outputs when only the reference changes.

Training Data

Around 2M LLM-augmented audios from 182,614 VGGSound clips times 11 source/context prompt variants, curated into 700K Stage 1 pairs and 71K Stage 2 contrastive pairs.
